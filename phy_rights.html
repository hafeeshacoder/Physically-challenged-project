Disability Rights & Legal Support

Empowering you with knowledge about your rights, legal support, and how to claim them.

📝 Rights of Persons with Disabilities (RPwD) Act, 2016

The RPwD Act, 2016 provides equal rights and opportunities for individuals with disabilities. It ensures protection from discrimination in education, employment, transport, and accessibility to public spaces. The law recognizes 21 types of disabilities and mandates inclusive education, job reservation, and social security benefits.

🪪 How to Apply for a Disability Certificate

Step 1: Visit the UDID Portal or your local government hospital.
Step 2: Submit medical documents and disability reports.
Step 3: Attend a medical board assessment (as per notification).
Step 4: Get the certificate digitally or physically after verification.

⚖️ Legal Aid & Helplines

  • 📞 National Disability Helpline: 1800-121-4509
  • 📩 Email Support: helpdesk@disabilityaffairs.gov.in
  • 🌐 Legal Services Authority: nalsa.gov.in
  • 🏛️ Disability Commissioner Office: Available in each state – visit district collector office for guidance.
