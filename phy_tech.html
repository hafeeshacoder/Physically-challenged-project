Assistive Technology Guides

Empower yourself with tools designed to improve your quality of life and independence.

Screen Readers

Screen readers convert text to speech or Braille, helping visually impaired users navigate content.

  • NVDA (NonVisual Desktop Access)
  • Apple VoiceOver
  • JAWS

Voice Recognition Software

Control your computer and write text using your voice.

  • Dictation.io
  • Google Voice Typing
  • Windows Voice Access

Mobility Aids

Devices like wheelchairs, walkers, and prosthetics that improve movement and independence.

Learn about modern mobility aids and their benefits from local healthcare providers.

Smartphone apps that help with navigation, communication, daily tasks, and health monitoring.

  • Seeing AI
