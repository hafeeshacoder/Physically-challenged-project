Tips for Coping with Social Stigma

  • 💡 Remember: Your worth is not defined by others’ opinions.
  • 💡 Surround yourself with supportive and understanding people.
  • 💡 Practice self-care: journaling, hobbies, or short walks.
  • 💡 Join groups or forums where you can share your journey.
  • 💡 Seek professional help if you feel overwhelmed—never hesitate.
